One-tap emergency alert that instantly notifies your trusted contacts and nearby authorities with your live location. Works even without internet.

Real-time navigation with community-verified safety scores. Avoid unsafe areas and find the safest path to your destination at any time of day.

Your safety network

Add up to 5 trusted contacts who receive your location and alerts automatically when SOS is triggered. They're always notified when you're in danger.
